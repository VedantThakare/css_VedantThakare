Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of cheese
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • Butter

Instructions:

1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of each slice of bread.

4. Place 2 slices of bacon and a few tomato slices on half of the bread slices.

5. Top with the remaining bread slices, cheese side down.

6. Place sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, and the cheese is melted.

7. Serve hot and enjoy!
